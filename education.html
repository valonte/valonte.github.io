EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

University of the Philippines - Diliman

Bachelor Science in Geodetic Engineering (2019)

For as long as I can remember, I have wanted to attend the University of the Philippines. With this desire, deciding where to attend college was simple. The University’s Geodetic Engineering departments is the only accredited Center of Excellence for Geodetic Engineering by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). My Bachelor's Program focused on the field of land surveying and mapping. The programs also exposed us to the fields of geodesy, remote sensing, photogrammetry, and geographic information systems (GIS). Studying at this prestigious and historic institution has had a profound impact on me in a variety of ways. If there is one thing I will never forget, it is the phrase "Honor before Excellence."

De La Salle University - Integrated School

High School Diploma (2014)

My elementary and high school years were spent in De La Salle University - Integrated School which is located in Laguna, Philippines. I was one of the first few students to enroll when the school first opened its doors. I thoroughly enjoyed my time with my friends, classmates and teachers, and I met the love of my life while attending this school. To cap it off, I also graduated sixth in my batch. As a result of this school’s science-oriented curriculum, I developed a strong interest in the sciences which drove me to pursue a degree in Geodetic Engineering.
